INTERNATIONAL CONFERENCE ON SMART COMPUTING, COMMUNICATION & AUTOMATION 2025 BY BUDGE BUDGE INSTITUTE OF TECHNOLOGY

WELCOME TO ICSCCA-2K25

The INTERNATIONAL CONFERENCE ON SMART COMPUTING, COMMUNICATION & AUTOMATION, (ICSCCA-2K25)  is aiming to provide a platform to researchers, scholars, engineers, scientists and industrial professionals to gather knowledge and bridge to fill the gap between academia and its industrial aspects, around the world. The conference will be an immersive experience primarily focusing on the latest advancements and researchers on various fields of engineering, including but not limited to Mechanical Engineering, Civil Engineering, Electrical Engineering, Electronics and Communications Engineering, Computer Science Engineering, Information Technology and other interdisciplinary areas.

The Conference objective is to foster collaboration, share innovations, and develop solutions for contemporary challenges. The platform of BBIT will allow discussions on regional and international issues related to engineering and IT, helping in the exchange of ideas to address modern societal challenges.

This conference is going to take place in Nischinatpur, Kolkata, West Bengal, INDIA. This conference is organized by the Budge Budge Institute of Technology, Kolkata,West Bengal, India. Best paper awards will be given to significant contributions during the conference.
